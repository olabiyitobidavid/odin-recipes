Fried Rice

Description

This is a dish commonly prepared by the West African people, especially in Nigeria.

Ingredient

  • Rice
  • Meat/Chicken/Turkey and stock
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Scotch bonnet (pepper)
  • Garlic & Ginger
  • Liver
  • Kidney beans
  • Butter
  • Sweet corn
  • Green pea
  • Green pepper
  • Carrot
  • Curry & Thyme
  • Salt and Seasonings
  • Kitchen foil
  • Crayfish
  • Bay leaf

Steps

  1. In a pot on fire, add stock.
  2. Add garlic & ginger.
  3. Add sliced onions.
  4. Add wahsed rice.
  5. Add liver, salt and seasonings.
  6. Add curry and thyme.
  7. Add butter, chopped pepper, carrots and scotch bonnet.
  8. Add little water if necessary.
  9. Cover with the kitchen foil to steam for some minutes.
  10. Add sweet corn, green peas and kidney beans.
  11. Repeat Step (9).
  12. Sprinkle grinded crayfish.
  13. Repeat Step (11).
  14. It should be ready within the next 15-25 minutes.
