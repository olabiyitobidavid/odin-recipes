Amala

Description

This is a local dish commonly prepared by the Yoruba people of Nigeria.

Ingredient

  • Yam flour
  • Turning stick
  • Your prefered stew (e.g., Ewedu or Egusi)

Steps

  1. Bring a water to boil in a pot.
  2. Add the flour and stir gently.
  3. Continue stiring thouroughly until it soldifies smoothly.
  4. It should be ready within the next 6-8 minutes.
