Akara

Description

This is a bean cake commonly prepared by the Yoruba people of Nigeria.

Ingredient

  • Bean flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • etc.

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add bean flour.
  2. Add chopped onions and grinded pepper.
  3. Add Tomato paste.
  4. Mix with some water and stir thorougly.
  5. In a frying pan on fire, add Vegetable oil.
  6. Using cooking spoon, put the mixed bean flour in the pan as many it can take.
  7. In every 1-2 minutes, using a cooking fork, turn the bean cake.
  8. etc.
