Jollof Rice

Description

This is a dish commonly prepared by the West African people, especially in Nigeria and Ghana.

Ingredient

  • Rice
  • Meat/Chicken/Turkey and stock.
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic & Ginger
  • Liver
  • Butter
  • Curry & Thyme
  • Salt and Seasonings
  • Kitchen foil
  • Crayfish
  • Bay leaf
  • Scotch bonnet (pepper), Red balls & Tomatoes

Steps

  1. In a pot on fire, add vegetable oil.
  2. Add garlic & ginger.
  3. Add Tomato paste and fry for about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Add all the pepper mix (scotch bonnet & red balls) and fry for few more minutes.
  5. Add salt & seasonings.
  6. Add curry, thyme and bay leaf.
  7. After few minutes, add the washed rice and stir gently.
  8. Add the stock and little water.
  9. Cover with the kitchen foil for some minutes.
  10. Check if there is need for water and add little at a time.
  11. Add butter, liver, grinded crayfish, onions and tomatoes.
  12. Cover again with the kitchen foil and allow to steam for some minutes.
  13. Repeat Step (10) if needed.
  14. It should be ready within the next 15-25 minutes.
