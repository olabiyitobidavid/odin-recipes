Jollof Rice

g

Description

This is a dish commonly prepared by the West African people, especially in Nigeria and Ghana.

Ingredient

  • Rice
  • Meat/Chicken/Turkey
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic & Ginger
  • Liver
  • Butter
  • Curry & Thyme
  • Salt and Seasonings
  • Kitchen foil
  • Crayfish
  • Bay leaf
  • Scotch bonnet, Read ball & Tomatoes
  • etc.

Steps

  1. In a pot on fire, add vegetable oil.
  2. Add garlic & ginger.
  3. Add Tomato paste and fry for about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Add all the pepper mix and fry for few more minutes.
  5. Add salt & seasonings.
  10. etc.
